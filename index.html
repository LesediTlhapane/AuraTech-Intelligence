Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between an AI Agent and a Workflow?

An AI Agent is a digital worker that handles a specific task—like answering customer questions or processing invoices. A Workflow connects multiple agents and systems together to automate an entire business process.

Do I need technical knowledge to use AI agents or workflows?

Not at all. Once deployed, your team interacts with the AI through simple chat interfaces or dashboard views. We handle all the technical setup, integration, and ongoing maintenance.

Who owns the AI agent and workflow after I pay?

You do. You retain full ownership of any custom AI agent or workflow built for your business.

How long does it take to build an AI agent or workflow?

Simple agents take 1-2 weeks. Complex agents with workflows and multiple integrations take 3-4 weeks.

Can I try an agent or workflow before committing?

Yes. The AI Scoping & Consultation includes a proof-of-concept demo showing exactly how an AI agent or workflow would work in your specific business.

What industries do you work with?

We work across industries—professional services, real estate, e-commerce, education, HR, manufacturing, and more.

What is the eStudy AI Incubation Programme?

It's a 12-month sponsored programme that supports entrepreneurs in building commercially viable AI agents. Lesedi is a current participant, and Aura Tech Intelligence is a direct outcome of this incubation.

Do you offer ongoing support after deployment?

Yes. We offer a 30-day post-launch support window, and you can also opt for our AI-as-a-Service retainer for continuous optimization and priority support.
